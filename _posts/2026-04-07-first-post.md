date: 2026-04-07 18:00:00 +0200

In machine design, simplicity is often a technical advantage rather than a limitation.

A simpler concept usually means:

- fewer parts  
- fewer failure points  
- easier assembly  
- lower production cost  
- better maintainability  

The goal is not complexity, but functionality with minimum system overhead.
